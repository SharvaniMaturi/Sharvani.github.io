Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology is an engineering college located in Gandipet,Hyderabad,Telangana.The college was affiliated to Osamania University and accrediated by National Board of Accrediation.

There are many departments in the college namely Department of Maths & Humanities, Dept.of Chemistry, Dept.of Physics, Dept.of CSE,Dept.of ECE, Dept.of EEE,Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering,Bio-technology,School of Management Studies,Production Engineering, Dept.of Information Technology.
